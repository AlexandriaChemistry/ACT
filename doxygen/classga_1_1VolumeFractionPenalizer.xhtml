Description

Penalizer that punishes the population if its volume is not large enough.

We create a parallelogram whose side lengths are defined by the maximum and minimum value of each parameter in the population. If the volume of the parallelogram divided by the total volume of the parameter space is smaller than a given fraction, we reinitialize a given fraction of the worst genomes.
